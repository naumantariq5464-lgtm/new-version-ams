Core Features

Everything You Need to Manage Attendance

From marking attendance to generating PDF reports — all in one secure, semester-isolated platform.

Smart Attendance Marking

Teachers select a date and subject, then mark each student as Present or Absent with one click. Records are saved instantly with full audit trail.

Real-Time Analytics

Students see subject-wise attendance percentages computed on-the-fly. Visualized with progress bars and clear color-coded indicators.

Monthly PDF Reports

Students can download a professionally formatted PDF report of their monthly attendance per subject with one click — no printing required.

JWT Security & RBAC

Every endpoint is protected by JWT tokens. Role-based access control ensures teachers and students only access their own authorized data.

Semester Isolation

Every record is strictly scoped to a semester. Teachers and students can never access cross-semester data — guaranteed at the database level.

Student Feedback System

Students can submit structured feedback (rating + comment) for their teachers — fostering accountability and continuous improvement.

How It Works

Simple Workflows for Both Roles

Teacher Flow
Full control over attendance & students
1
Login with credentials

Teacher logs in with their pre-seeded email and password. A JWT token is issued upon successful authentication.

2
Complete profile setup

On first login, teacher fills in their Name, Subject, Class, Semester, and Department to unlock full access.

3
Add students & subjects

Teacher enrolls students into their semester and creates subjects they teach — all strictly isolated to their account.

4
Mark daily attendance

Select a date and subject, mark each student as Present or Absent, and submit. Records can be updated or deleted anytime.

Student Flow
View attendance, feedback & PDF download
1
Register an account

Students self-register with their name, email, password, semester, and department. No admin approval needed.

2
Login & access dashboard

After login, students land on their personalized dashboard showing all attendance data for their semester.

3
View subject-wise attendance

See Present/Absent counts, attendance percentage, and teacher name for each subject in a clear table view.

4
Download PDF & submit feedback

Download monthly attendance PDF or submit a rating and comment as feedback for any of their teachers.

User Roles

Built for Two Roles

Every feature, permission, and data boundary is designed around precise role definitions.

👨‍🏫
Teacher

Teachers are pre-seeded by administrators and have full ownership over their semester data — no teacher can access another's records.

  • Mark attendance per date and subject
  • Add, manage, delete students in their semester
  • Create and manage subjects they teach
  • Update or delete their own attendance records
  • Edit their own profile information
